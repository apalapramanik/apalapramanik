## 🔬 Research Overview

My research lies at the intersection of **millimeter-wave (mmWave) wireless communication**,  
**beamforming**, and **machine learning**, with a strong emphasis on **real-world, hardware-in-the-loop experimentation**.

I focus on designing **robust, adaptive beam management frameworks** that integrate
vision, sensing, and learning to improve reliability in highly dynamic environments.

---

## 🧠 Research Interests
- Millimeter-wave (mmWave) communication systems  
- Vision-assisted beamforming and beam tracking  
- Reinforcement learning for joint beam & power control  
- V2X and dynamic outdoor wireless channels  
- Hardware-in-the-loop experiments (24–60 GHz)

---

## 🚧 Current Work
- Camera-primed **double-directional beam management**
- RL-based adaptive beamforming and power control 
- Modeling environmental effects on mmWave channel dynamics
